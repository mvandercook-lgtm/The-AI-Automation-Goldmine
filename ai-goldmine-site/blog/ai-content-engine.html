How to Build a $10K/mo AI Content Engine in 30 Days

MV
M. VanderCook
·June 10, 2025 ·8 min read

Three solo founders. Zero employees. A combined $340K in annual revenue from content alone. What is their secret? A precisely engineered AI content engine that produces, distributes, and monetizes content at a scale that used to require a full editorial team.

This is not theory. These are the exact tools and workflows — battle-tested and revenue-verified.

⚡ TL;DR: GPT-4o generates content → Notion organizes → Buffer distributes → ConvertKit monetizes. The full loop runs in under 2 hours per week once set up.

The Stack (What You Actually Need)

  • GPT-4o / Claude 3.5 Sonnet — Content generation and ideation
  • Notion — Content calendar and asset management
  • Buffer / Hypefury — Social media scheduling and analytics
  • ConvertKit — Email list monetization and automation
  • Perplexity AI — Research and trend identification
  • n8n.io — Gluing everything together automatically

Phase 1: Topic Mining (Week 1)

The biggest mistake content creators make is guessing what their audience wants. The AI content engine starts with data, not hunches.

The Perplexity Research Loop

Every Monday, run this prompt in Perplexity AI with Focus: Web enabled:

What are the top 10 questions entrepreneurs are asking about [YOUR NICHE]
right now in 2025? Include questions from Reddit, Quora, LinkedIn, and Twitter.
Format as: Question | Platform | Estimated Monthly Search Volume | Pain Score (1-10)

This surfaces real, urgent questions your audience is actively searching for — feeding a week's worth of content briefs in minutes.

Phase 2: Content Production (Week 2)

With your topic list ready, the production workflow kicks in. Do not think of this as "writing with AI" — think of it as directing an editorial team.

The 3-Layer Content System

  • Layer 1 — Pillar Post: 2,500-word SEO article (GPT-4o)
  • Layer 2 — Social Atoms: 10 LinkedIn posts distilled from the pillar
  • Layer 3 — Email Digest: Weekly newsletter summary for your list

Phase 3: Distribution Automation (Week 3)

Production without distribution is a hobby. The engine's real power is in automated distribution via n8n workflows that post, schedule, and track everything without manual intervention.

Phase 4: Monetization (Week 4)

Revenue follows attention. With a growing content footprint, monetization layers in naturally through affiliate partnerships, digital products, and newsletter sponsorships — all managed through ConvertKit automation sequences.

🔑 Key Insight: The founders hitting $10K/mo are not producing 10x more content — they are distributing each piece 10x more effectively using automation.

Your 30-Day Action Plan

  • Days 1–7: Set up your Notion content calendar and run your first Perplexity research loop
  • Days 8–14: Produce your first 4 pillar posts and 40 social atoms
  • Days 15–21: Configure n8n automation and Buffer scheduling
  • Days 22–30: Launch ConvertKit welcome sequence and first monetization offer

The engine does not happen overnight — but by Day 30, you will have a system that compounds. Month 2 is where the economics shift dramatically.
